BMI Categories
Where do you fall?
BMI is a widely-used first indicator of health. Each range comes with specific dietary and lifestyle guidance.
🫙
Underweight
Below 18.5
May need a calorie-rich, protein-dense diet to reach a healthy weight.
✅
Normal weight
18.5 – 24.9
Maintain your balanced diet and active lifestyle to stay in this range.
⚠️
Overweight
25 – 29.9
A low-calorie, fibre-rich diet and regular exercise can help restore balance.
🔴
Obese
30 and above
Professional guidance alongside structured dietary changes is recommended.
